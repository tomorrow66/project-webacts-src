Most major religions support organ donation.

For example, in the Islam faith, “It is permissible to transplant an organ from a dead person to a living person whose life or basic essential functions depend on that organ, subject to the condition that permission be given by the deceased before his death, or by his heirs after his death….”

To inform your decision, consider prayer and discussing donation with your faith leader.

There are various biblical scriptures that can be applied to organ donation such as, “…It is more blessed to give than to receive” Acts 20:35 and “I have no silver or gold but what I have I give to you in the name of Jesus Christ.” – Acts 3:6

The bible also says that the spirit goes to heaven, not the body.

After exploring this website and becoming informed about the facts, think about registering on your state website to give the most precious gift of all – the gift of life.
