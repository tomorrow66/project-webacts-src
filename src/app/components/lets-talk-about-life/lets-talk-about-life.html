Organ donation saves lives!

Every 10 minutes a new name is added to the national organ waiting list. And every day, about 21 people in the United States die while waiting for a transplant of a vital organ, such as a heart, liver, kidney, pancreas, lung or bone marrow. Just remember, one organ donor can save 8 lives.

Whether or not you should donate is your decision. If you decide to be a donor, indicate your intent on your driver’s license and tell your family.


After exploring this website and becoming informed about the facts, think about registering on your state website to give the most precious gift of all – the gift of life.
