In the case of an accident, your life is the number one priority!

The team that works to save your life is completely different from the organ transplant team. The team that works to save your life does not check your license before they begin caring for you. The organ transplant team doesn’t come until after death has been declared.

The computerized system that selects organ recipients is based on the severity of illness, time spent waiting, blood type and geographic location. Wealth, race and gender are not considered.*
