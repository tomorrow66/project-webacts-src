In the case of an accident, your life is the number one priority!

No healthcare worker checks if you are an organ donor before they begin caring for you. The hospital team that works to save your life is completely different from the organ transplant team.

A computer system selects a person on the waiting list to receive and organ based on the severity of illness, time spent waiting, blood type and geographic location. Wealth, race and gender are not considered. For more information*
