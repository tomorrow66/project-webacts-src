Most major religions support organ donation.*

Christianity

There are various biblical scriptures that can be applied to organ donation such as, “…It is more blessed to give than to receive” - Acts 20:35 and “I have no silver or gold but what I have I give to you in the name of Jesus Christ.” - Acts 3:6

Catholicism

According to Pope John Paul II, “transplants are morally and ethically acceptable to the Vatican.”*

Judaism

The Conservative Movement's Committee on Jewish Laws and Standards stated that organ donation is an act of kindness that saves lives.*

Islam

The Fourth Conference of the Islamic Fiqh Council determined, “It is permissible to transplant an organ from a dead person to a living person whose life or basic essential functions depend on that organ, subject to the condition that permission be given by the deceased before his death, or by his heirs after his death…”*

To inform your decision, consider prayer and discussing donation with your faith leader.
